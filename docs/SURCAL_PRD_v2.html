__SURCAL__ Product Requirements Document Version 2\.0 — Updated March 2026 Scotty Reed | Built on Glide \+ Make \+ Claude API \+ Stripe __Platform__ Glide __AI__ Claude API __Automation__ Make __Payments__ Stripe Connect # __1\. Product Overview__ SURCAL \(pronounced like 'circle'\) is a reverse marketplace — buyers post what they need, sellers compete by submitting bids, and Claude AI automatically scores every bid so buyers see the best options first\. The name came from taking the word supercalifragilisticexpialidocious and removing the letters that didn't fit\. SURCAL works for anything: freelance work, physical products, local services, creative projects, custom orders\. If you need it and someone can provide it, SURCAL is the fastest way to connect the two\. The platform takes a 5% fee on every completed transaction\. Stripe Connect handles the payment split automatically — sellers receive 95% directly to their bank account, SURCAL keeps 5%\. # __2\. Problem Statement__ Traditional marketplaces make buyers do all the work\. You browse hundreds of listings, compare options manually, reach out to sellers one by one, and hope someone gets back to you at a reasonable price\. Sellers compete in a feed where visibility depends on paid promotion rather than quality\. SURCAL flips this\. Buyers post once and sellers come to them\. Claude AI does the quality filtering automatically so buyers never have to read through bad bids\. The best bid wins — not the one that paid for placement\. # __3\. Brand__ ## __3\.1 Name__ SURCAL\. User Personas__ ## __4\.1 The Buyer__ Anyone who needs a product or service and wants competitive pricing without doing the searching themselves\. They post a request, sit back, and review AI\-scored bids\. They don't search, browse, or reach out to anyone — sellers come to them\. Buyers can attach a photo to their request so sellers immediately understand what's needed\. This is especially useful for physical items, repairs, or anything visual\. ## __4\.2 The Seller__ A freelancer, service provider, or vendor who wants to find clients actively looking for what they offer\. Instead of spending money on ads or competing in a crowded listing feed, they browse open buyer requests and submit targeted bids\. They see their own AI score so they learn what makes a winning bid over time\. # __5\. Tech Stack__ Decided during Spring Break 2026 — switched from Xano \+ Softr to Glide \+ Make for simplicity\. AI Features \(Claude API via Make\)__ Every AI feature runs through Make automations that call the Claude API\. Glide triggers the automation via webhook, Make calls Claude, Claude returns JSON, Make saves the result back to Glide's data table\. __Feature__ __Trigger__ __Claude prompt goal__ __Output saved to__ Bid scoring Bid submitted Score 1–10 \+ reason \+ flagged boolean Bids table: ai\_score, ai\_reason, flagged Fraud flagging Bid submitted \(same call\) Flag if vague, spammy, or suspicious Bids table: flagged column Request improvement Request posted Rewrite description to be clearer \+ more specific Requests table: ai\_description column ## __8\.1 Bid scoring prompt__ Score this seller bid 1\-10\.
