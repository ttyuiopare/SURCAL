1. Overview

SURCAL is a reverse marketplace where buyers post requests for products or services and sellers compete with bids to win their business. Unlike traditional marketplaces that are built for sellers, SURCAL puts buyers in control ΓÇö setting terms, budgets, and timelines while sellers compete on price, quality, and trust.

Vision

To become the default platform for buyer-initiated commerce, where every purchase starts with what the buyer needs ΓÇö not what the seller has listed.

Target Users

  • Buyers: Individuals and small businesses looking for specific products/services at competitive prices
  • Sellers: Freelancers, agencies, and vendors who want to acquire customers by competing on merit rather than advertising spend

3. Key User Flows

Buyer Flow

  1. Sign up and confirm email
  2. Post a request (category, description, budget, deadline)
  3. Receive bids from sellers with AI trust scores visible
  4. Compare bids side-by-side using AI Price Advisor insights
  5. Accept winning bid ΓåÆ funds held in Stripe escrow
  6. Confirm delivery ΓåÆ funds released to seller

Seller Flow

  1. Sign up and complete profile
  2. Browse open requests by category
  3. Place a bid with price, delivery estimate, and message
  4. AI Smart Match surfaces relevant requests
  5. If bid accepted ΓåÆ fulfill the request
  6. Payment released after buyer confirmation

4. Technical Architecture

Frontend

React 18 (single-file HTML), Glassmorphism UI, dark theme with magenta accent (#E5007D)

Backend

Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, Realtime, Edge Functions, Row Level Security)

Payments

Stripe (escrow via payment intents, subscription billing, webhooks)

AI Layer

Client-side intelligence with category-based market data models

Hosting

Vercel (static deployment, edge CDN)

Design System

Bebas Neue + DM Sans fonts, magenta / AI purple (#8B5CF6), glassmorphism cards

Database Schema (Key Tables)

  • profiles ΓÇö user accounts linked to Supabase Auth
  • requests ΓÇö buyer posts with category, budget, deadline, status
  • bids ΓÇö seller offers linked to requests with price and message
  • categories ΓÇö 8 predefined marketplace categories
  • transactions ΓÇö Stripe payment records with escrow status
  • subscription_plans ΓÇö Free / Pro / Business tier definitions
  • user_subscriptions ΓÇö active user subscription records
  • pricing_waitlist ΓÇö pre-launch interest capture for paid tiers

Transaction Fees

A 5% escrow fee is charged on every accepted bid. Stripe handles secure payment processing, holding funds in escrow until the buyer confirms delivery. This protects both parties and generates per-transaction revenue that scales with marketplace volume.
